# License notices for the OMOP sample

The sample data is the [OHDSI Eunomia GiBleed_5.3](https://github.com/OHDSI/EunomiaDatasets/tree/main/datasets/GiBleed) dataset, distributed by OHDSI under the [Apache License 2.0](https://github.com/OHDSI/EunomiaDatasets/blob/main/LICENSE).

The Eunomia dataset includes curated subsets of standardized vocabulary terms from SNOMED, RxNorm, LOINC, and other vocabularies via the [OHDSI Athena](https://athena.ohdsi.org/) project. Concept names are reproduced here for analytical readability under those vocabularies' redistribution terms; please attribute OHDSI Athena and the individual source vocabularies in any derivative work.

The Eunomia data is generated synthetically (originally seeded from [Synthea](https://github.com/synthetichealth/synthea)) and contains no real patient information.

The Malloy semantic model (`omop.malloy`) and the notebooks in this directory are released under the same [MIT License](../LICENSE) as the rest of `malloy-samples`.
